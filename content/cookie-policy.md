## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit — such as your language preference or consent choices — to improve your experience on subsequent visits.

This website uses cookies in accordance with the **GDPR (Regulation EU 2016/679)**, the Italian **D.Lgs. 196/2003** (Privacy Code), and the guidelines issued by the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante Privacy).

## 2. Categories of cookies used

### 2.1 Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function correctly. They do not require your consent.

| Name | Domain | Purpose | Duration |
|------|--------|---------|----------|
| `cc_cookie` | damager.eu | Stores your cookie consent preferences | 12 months |

### 2.2 Analytics cookies (opt-in)

These cookies are set by **Google Analytics 4** only if you accept the "Analytics" category in the cookie banner. IP anonymisation is always enabled.

| Name | Domain | Purpose | Duration |
|------|--------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | .damager.eu | Distinguishes unique users | 2 years |
| `_ga_*` | .damager.eu | Maintains session state for GA4 | 2 years |
| `_gid` | .damager.eu | Distinguishes users (short-term) | 24 hours |

### 2.3 Functional cookies (opt-in)

The interactive consortium map uses **Leaflet.js** with **OpenStreetMap** tiles. These are loaded only if you accept the "Functional" category. OpenStreetMap does not set persistent tracking cookies in your browser; however, loading map tiles sends your IP address to the OpenStreetMap tile servers, which are covered by the [OSM Foundation Privacy Policy](https://osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy).

| Name | Domain | Purpose | Duration |
|------|--------|---------|----------|
| *(none set by browser)* | tile.openstreetmap.org | Map tile requests — server-side log only, no cookie stored | Session |

## 4. How to manage your preferences

### 4.1 Cookie banner

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies at any time using the **Cookie Preferences** button in the footer of every page.

### 4.2 Browser settings

You can also manage cookies directly through your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of this website.

### 4.3 Google Analytics opt-out

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites, you can install the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### 4.4 OpenStreetMap tiles

Map tile requests go to `tile.openstreetmap.org`. OpenStreetMap does not set tracking cookies; requests are covered by the [OSM Foundation Privacy Policy](https://osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy). You can disable tile loading at any time by withdrawing functional consent via the Cookie Preferences button in the footer.
